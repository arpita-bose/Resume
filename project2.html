Visualized the scenario of Suicide Cases in India

  • Visit

Suicide is a miserable act that takes the life of an individual. It is a tragedy that affects the members of their family, their friends and community.

In India, every year more than 1,00,000 people ends their own life by commiting suicide. The data used in this survey provides the overview from the year 2000 to 2020 for all the States & UT in India.

  • Technology Used: Python Streamlit, Python Pandas, MS-Excel
